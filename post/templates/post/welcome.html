About Business & Tenders

We are an online supplier sourcing platform that provides businesses with procurement processes by identifying bidding activities like Tender adverts, prequalification adverts, Ecpressions of Interest and Request for Proposals
We not only provide business with supplier information but also provide career, jobs and opportunities for every one

What we offer virtually for you

Bidding Process

Bidding process revolves around the sourcing for bid adverts for instance tender, perqualification, Expression of Interest and RFQ adverts that mainly appear in newspapers or online bid sourcing platform.
A tender is a procurement type of bidding which is specific to the requirements or specifications offered by the end use/ client. Tenders are run for a specific project and evaluated within a short period of time.
Prequalification or expressions of interest are conducted in order to run cheks or verifications of the company such as the company exist, years in operation,experience and validity of operation documnents

Jobs/careers & opportunities

In the day to day world, apply for a job under your area of expertise and passion leads you to your dream job which is for a short term period. With this we help provide you with a platform that advertises jobs that have been verified and proven by the sourcing compaies
